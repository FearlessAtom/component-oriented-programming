Interpretation

2. Collecting and Using Your Personal Data
---------------------------------------

Types of Data Collected
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Personal Data
*************
While using Our Service, We do not require You to provide any personally identifiable information. Our Service does not support user accounts or registration. We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Usage Data & Tracking Technologies
*********************************
We use Cookies to track activity on Our Service and store certain information to improve Your experience. These include:

* Necessary / Essential Cookies: These are vital for the game to function. They handle basic session state and ensure the game logic remains stable during your visit.
* Functionality / Preference Cookies: These allow Us to remember choices You make (such as the number of cards on the board or move limits). This ensures You don't have to re-enter Your preferences every time You play.
* Analytics Cookies: These collect anonymous data on how the game is used, allowing us to balance difficulty levels and improve gameplay.


3. Data Retention and Deletion
---------------------------

Retention
~~~~~~~~~
Since We do not utilize a central database for user profiles, Your data is stored locally on Your device. We do not retain Your Personal Data on Our servers.

* Local Cookies: These remain on Your device for up to 150 days or until You manually clear Your browser cache.

Your Rights (GDPR)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Under GDPR, even without a user account, You have the right to:
1. Request Information: Know exactly what data is being stored (which is limited to the categories in our Cookie Settings).
2. Delete Data: You can delete all data We "hold" simply by clearing Your browser cookies or using the "Reject All" option in our Cookie Settings banner.
3. Withdraw Consent: You can change Your mind about Analytics or Preference cookies at any time via the settings menu.


4. Disclosure of Your Data
-----------------------
We do not sell, rent, or share Your Personal Data with third parties for marketing purposes. Because we do not collect names or emails, there is no database of user information to be transferred or sold.

We may only disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency) regarding IP addresses found in standard server logs.


5. Security of Your Personal Data
------------------------------
The security of Your data is important to Us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We use standard web encryption to protect the delivery of the Service, but we encourage You to manage Your own browser security settings.


6. Children's Privacy
